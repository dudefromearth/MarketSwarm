**Modules Overview**:

- **Module 1: Introduction to Tail-Risk Hedging**

  - Examples: SPX, CL, NQ trades.

- **Module 5: Trading Psychology**  
  Prepares traders for low win rates (<10%) and the emotional discipline required for asymmetry-based strategies.  
  - Focus: Journaling, reflection, handling losses.

- **Module 6: Historical Case Studies**  
  Analyzes past tail events (e.g., 2008, COVID-19) and how the Fat Tail Protocol would have performed.

- **Module 7: Advanced Strategies**  
  Introduces blending Fat Tail Protocol with other strategies, integrating AI tools, and customizing setups for different market conditions.  
  - Topics: AI prompts, risk stacking, strategy evolution.

**Core Principles**:
- Convexity: Small known risks, large potential upside.
- Optionality: Trade setups that don't require prediction.
- Patience + Discipline: Embrace small losses, focus on long-term asymmetry.
