## 1. Purpose & Doctrine

### 1.1 What a Playbook *Is*

A Playbook is **distilled lived knowledge**.

Not:
- A strategy recipe  
- A ruleset  
- A checklist  
- A tutorial  

It *is*:
- A crystallization of repeated experience  
- A container for wisdom discovered, not prescribed  
- A reusable lens the trader can return to  

> Playbooks are **written after reflection**, not during action.

---

### 1.2 Guiding Doctrine

The Playbook surface must honor:

- **Object â†’ Reflection â†’ Action**
- **Antifragility** (learning from variance, not optimizing it away)
- **Presence over performance**
- **Extraction, not invention**

The system must never ask:

> â€œWhat should your playbook say?â€

It must only ever ask:

> â€œWhat keeps appearing?â€

## 5. Authoring Model

### 5.1 No Blank Page

The authoring surface never opens empty.

Initial prompt:

> *â€œHere are the moments you marked. What connects them?â€*

---

### 5.2 Integrative Writing Only

The user does **not** generate from scratch.

They may:
- Pull excerpts into the surface
- Highlight phrases
- Rewrite in their own words
- Collapse many observations into one insight

Supported actions:
- Quoting
- Rephrasing
- Merging
- Annotating

---

## 6. Playbook Structure (Soft, Not Rigid)

### 6.1 Canonical Sections (Optional)

Each Playbook *may* include:

1. **Context**  
   *When does this matter?*
2. **Signal / Recognition**  
   *How do I notice it?*
3. **Response**  
   *What do I do when I see it?*
4. **Failure Mode**  
   *How does this break?*
5. **Notes**  
   Open reflection

None are required.  
All are lenses, not steps.

---

### 6.2 No Ordering Pressure

Sections may be:
- Empty
- Partial
- Reordered
- Removed

A Playbook may be one paragraph or many pages.

## 10. Downstream Use of Playbooks

### 10.1 Where Playbooks Apply

Playbooks may inform:

- Vexy reflections

## 11. Anti-Patterns (Hard Rules)

The Playbook surface must never:

- Gamify creation
- Score quality
- Track completion
- Rank Playbooks
- Encourage volume over depth

If a trader creates **one Playbook in a year**
and it changes how they trade,
the system succeeded.
